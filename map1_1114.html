Winneba is a fishing village located on the Gulf of Guinea. Today, Winneba is seeking to transform from a tribal society to a modern one. As Many other African cultures, Winneba has a tradition of oral historian, which document history by story-telling. Here's a collective history map of people's everyday life in Winneba.
